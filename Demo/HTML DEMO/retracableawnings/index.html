What are the types of retractable & patio awnings?

Retractable

  • - Patio and deck lateral arm awnings, pergola covers, retractable louvered roofs, rotating louver roofs, canopies, and side or drop arm awnings.
  • - Screens (vertical awnings).
  • - Freestanding.
  • - Electric, motorized, automated or manual operation.
  • - Fabric canopies or glass louvers or polycarbonate.

Stationary

  • - Usually permanently attached to a building.
  • - Porch, balcony, deck, or window dressing or protection.
  • - Fixed frame.
  • - Wooden, metal, acrylic, polycarbonate or glass roofs or glass louvers or fabric canopies.Freestanding.

Freestanding

  • - Often attached to a deck or patio, but can be portable (varies by product type).
  • - Pergola, trellis, gazebo, vehicle shelters, outdoor kitchen or BBQ shelters.
  • - Fixed or retractable canopy.
  • - Need to be securely anchored due to wind.
  • - The frame is aluminum, metal, wood or PVC; cover is often wood, fabric, glass, metal, polycarbonate, aluminum or fabric.

Who needs a retractable awning?

Homeowners and business can benefit from the best retractable awnings. Whether attached to their building or freestanding, retractable products offer protection when needed, but can disappear when not required.

The product protects you or your client from the environment but can be easily stored to protect it from the environment too.
